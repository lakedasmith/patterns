Body Panel (make 2, font and back)

Ch 80.
Row 1) HDC in 2nd ch from hook (79)
Row 2-45) Ch 1, turn, hdc across.
*optional: stripes (rows 36-40) – switch to cc for 5 rows

Front Neck Shaping (only one panel)

Mark center 17 sts (for v-neck shaping)
From Row 35 (approx.) begin v-neck
Row 36) work to center marker, dec 1, turn
Rows 37-45) Dec 1 at neck edge every row
FO and repeat on other side

Join Shoulders

With RS facing, sl st or sew 18 sts at each shoulder. Leave neck space for center.

Sleeves (2)

Row 1) Ch 46. HDC in 2nd ch from hook and across
2-37) ch 1, turn, hdc across.
**increase every 4 rows, add 1 st at each end. Continue to Row 30.
**Add stripes: work 3 rows of CC mid-sleeve or near cuff.
FO. Fold in half and sew underarm seam.

Assembly

1) Sew front and back together at sides, leaving armholes (approx. 7.5-8” deep)
2) Attach sleeves into armholes
3) Weave in all ends

V-Neck Shaping

Neck border:
w/MC, attach at shoulder seam
Row 1) Sc around neckline
Row 2) Sc around, dec at v-point for shaping. FO.

String Tie:

Ch 90. Weave through neckline round (v-shaped) like a lace-up.
**optional: add tassels or crochet cord ends.
